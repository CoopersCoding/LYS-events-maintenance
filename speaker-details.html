Speaker Details

I am your friendly neighborhood Matchmaker, Dating Coach, Relationship Coach, Love Coach, your new BFF, your wing-woman.

Jackie Dorman

Jackie Dorman is a relationship coach, professional matchmaker, and speaker who has been empowering men and women to live their best lives for decades. She is a regular contributor on national radio, reaching millions with her humorous, no-nonsense, and relatable style.

Jackie's breakthrough mentoring program for dating and relationships, "Last Year Single", has become an international movement that has helped thousands of singles find love and rediscover hope and healing for their lives in a vibrant community. Jackie calls Austin, Texas, home with her husband, David. Together they are proud parents of a blended family that includes three adult children.
