F.A.Q.

  • What's not included?

    The following are not included in the packages:


    • Flights/Visas
    • Hotel accommodations are not provided unless you purchased the VIP package. See the hotel section for a list of hotels reccommended by Last Trip Single.
    • Travel insurance, yet is highly recommended
    • Transportation for personal excursions (outside of the Last Trip Single One Day Trip Experiences where shuttle is provided)
    • Meals not mentioned in the inclusion
    • Spending/shopping money
  • When is the event?

    • Last Trip Single retreat is June 27th
    • Recommended arrival date is June 26th

    • Last Year Single conference is June 28th - 29th
    • Note: Recommended departure date is June 30th
  • Where is the conference being held?

    The Last Year Single conference is being held at Winsport Canada Olympic Park in the Legacy room.

  • Are transportation arrangements available?

    If you purchased the VIP package or one/both of the Day Trip experiences, shuttles are included for a few of the designated group outings.

    Transportation from the airport to the accommodation of your choice is not included. You have a variety of options available to get around during your stay, such as public transit, Ubers, taxis, shuttles, rental cars, and limousines.

  • Are there any organized shuttles?

    If you purchased the VIP package or one/both of the Day Trip experiences, shuttles are included for a few of the designated group outings.

    There are also several other options available to get around during your stay, including public transit, Ubers, taxis, shuttles, rental cars, and limousines.

  • What is the dress code?

    We want you to be comfortable during the retreat and conference. You will want to consider the weather in the locations and outings you are joining us for.

  • What is the expected weather?

    Canmore, Canada in June is typically a high of 66 F /18 C and low of 42 F / 5 C. Temperatures can get a little lower in higher altitudes of the Canadian Rockies and the chance of rain increases in June so you may want to bring a light, waterproof rain jacket or windbreaker. If you plan to go for a hike, be sure to bring comfortable shoes.

    Banff, Canada in June is typically a high of 65 F / 18 C and a low of 41 F / 5 C. Temperatures can get a little lower in Banff (Canadian Rockies) at night so you may want to bring a long sleeve Shirt/warmer jacket. If you plan to go for a hike, be sure to bring comfortable shoes.

  • What food will be served at the event?

    If you purchased the VIP package or one/both of the Day Trip experiences, a group dinner is included during the Banff day trip.

    If you purchased the VIP package or the In-person conference package, lunch is provided on Saturday, June 29th.

    There are a wide variety of food options which are available on Main Street in Banff to choose which can also accommodate any food restrictions or allergies.

  • When can I register?

    Our registration is online and individuals can register/buy tickets while the registration is open through this website.

  • Can I register the day of the event?

    Due to planning needs for the VIP Experience and Day Trip Experiences, we will not be able to take last minute registrations.

    You may be able to register for the conference the day of the event if it’s not sold out, but lunch will not be included for last minute registrations.

  • How do I pay for the registration?

    Individuals who register for the event will pay through our secure payment system using the register/buy tickets button on this website.

  • What happens if the event is canceled due to weather conditions?

    Ticket processing fees can not be refunded in the event of cancellation. Last Year Single is not liable for refunds on airline tickets, car rentals, etc. Individuals would need to work directly with the airlines and car rental companies for those reimbursements.

    NOTE: We have alternate plans in place should there be any weather issues for the conference as well as during the retreat. Conference ticket price is non-refundable, however, can be transferred to another.

  • Do I need travel insurance?

    Travel insurance is not mandatory, yet it is highly recommended.

  • Where is the Last Trip Single Liability Waiver?

    Click this LINK to access the waiver. The waiver must be signed by participants in the Calgary June 2024 VIP Retreat and the Day Trip to Banff.

Event Information

Here is a brief description of events

Banff Day Trip

Spend the day exploring the incredible scenery of the Canadian Rockies during this day trip from Calgary to the mountain town of Banff and its surrounding attractions. Check out the surreal rock formations near Tunnel Mountain Drive, shaped by thousands of years of wind and rain. Then soar above Banff on board the Banff Gondola as you take in incredible views of the town and surrounding peaks. At the conclusion of the tour, you have the option of being dropped off in Banff for your own leisure before your shuttle departs back to Calgary.
