Welcome to Sandman Hotel & Suites Calgary West

The perfect soft landing at the end of your day. For immediate fun right outside our door, we’re located across from WinSport. It’s best known as the ski jumping, luge, and bobsledding venue of the 1988 Winter Olympics, and where Eddie the Eagle famously captured the hearts of sports fans everywhere. If you love skiing, outdoor activities and quick access to the Canadian Rocky Mountains, then Sandman Hotel & Suites Calgary West is the jumping-off point for all of the above and more.
